1 Plantuml

1.1 Installation

Put plantuml.jar in ~/bin. The path setting is in emacs, use babel to evaluate.

  • Extension: <|--
  • Composition: *--
  • Aggregation: o--

Use .. instead of -- to have dotted line.

It is possible to add labels at three positions: the two ends of the relation, and the middle. Use extension as example:

Use in emacs: 

\#+BEGIN_SRC plantuml :file wikitmp_plantuml.png :exports results
